    Our service

    Sandoval's Catering provides quality foods and exceptional service for your event needs. Whether it's a school luncheon, birthday celebration or formal dinner, Sandoval's has the cuisine for all occasions. We offer a wide variety of fresh foods that are never frozen or pre-ordered. Almost all of our foods are original recipes and house-made daily.

    Our catering service includes complete setup and clean up of food and drinks for your event. Buffet style meals are served in chafing dishes with serving utensils unless requested otherwise. Linens, place settings and full-service caterers are available upon request for an additional cost. Decorations and centerpieces are also available upon request for an additional cost and with advanced notice. A 20% delivery, setup and clean up fee will be added based on the total cost of prepared food.
